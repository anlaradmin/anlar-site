About ANLAR

Building Africa's Learning Analytics Community

The African Network for Learning Analytics Research (ANLAR) brings together researchers, practitioners, and institutions across Africa working in Learning Analytics. Formally launched on 13 December 2022 as a Special Interest Group (SIG) of the Society for Learning Analytics Research (SoLAR), ANLAR advances research, practice, and dialogue on Learning Analytics within the African context while strengthening connections to the broader international community.


Learning Analytics is an evolving field with growing relevance for higher education and digital learning in Africa. ANLAR provides a platform to connect African scholars, foster collaboration, and increase visibility and participation within the global LA community.

Our Goals

What We Aim to Achieve

Grow the Research Community

Build an active group of Learning Analytics researchers on the African continent, creating a strong network of scholars and practitioners.

Enable Knowledge Exchange

Facilitate knowledge exchange between African researchers and institutions and the international Learning Analytics community.

What We Do

Our Activities

Organise meetings and workshops to explore collaboration opportunities and knowledge exchange across the continent.

Promote institutional awareness of Learning Analytics applications, opportunities, and ethical considerations.

Support applications for international research funding for collaborative Learning Analytics projects.

Share scholarly resources, datasets, and research opportunities among members.

Encourage participation in international Learning Analytics events such as LAK, LASI, and related SoLAR initiatives.
